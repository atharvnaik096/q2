Shipping Information

At Our Quickcart Store, we strive to provide fast and reliable shipping for all our customers. Please review the shipping policies and details below to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

Shipping Costs

We offer competitive shipping rates based on your location and the size of your order:

  • Standard Shipping: Free on orders above $50. For orders under $50, a flat rate of $5.99 applies.
  • Express Shipping: Available at $14.99 for faster delivery (2-3 business days).
  • Overnight Shipping: $24.99 for next-day delivery in select locations.

Delivery Time

All orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. Delivery time varies depending on your selected shipping method:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day (order must be placed before 2 PM).

Please note that processing times may be delayed during holidays or peak seasons.

International Shipping

We also ship internationally! Shipping fees and delivery times will vary depending on your country. Please check the estimated delivery time at checkout.

  • International Standard Shipping: 10-15 business days.
  • International Express Shipping: 5-7 business days.

Note: Customs fees or import duties may apply, which are the responsibility of the customer.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email confirmation with tracking information. You can track your order directly through our website or via the carrier's website using the provided tracking number.

Shipping Restrictions

Unfortunately, we do not ship to P.O. Boxes or military APO/FPO addresses at this time. Some oversized items may have additional shipping restrictions based on location.

Shipping Delays

While we strive to meet all estimated delivery times, certain factors such as weather conditions, holidays, or unexpected events may cause delays. In such cases, we will do our best to notify you of any changes to your delivery.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding shipping or need assistance with your order, feel free to contact our Customer Service team.

Returns & Refunds Policy

At Quickcart, we strive to provide the best shopping experience for our customers. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help. Please review our returns and refunds policy below.

1. Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days from the date of delivery.
  • The item must be in its original packaging, unused, and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Items such as perishable goods, intimate apparel, and customized products are non-returnable.
  • Proof of purchase or receipt is required to complete the return.

2. Process for Returns

  1. Contact our customer support team via email at support@quickcart.com to initiate a return.
  2. Provide your order number, reason for return, and supporting images if necessary.
  3. We will review your request and provide you with return shipping instructions.
  4. Once the item is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

3. Refunds

If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment.

  • Credit card refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear in your account, depending on your card issuer.
  • In some cases, a partial refund may be granted for items that show signs of use or are missing parts.
  • If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days, please contact your bank and then reach out to us.

4. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item for the same product, please contact us at support@quickcart.com.

5. Shipping for Returns

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

6. Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Perishable goods such as food, flowers, newspapers, or magazines
  • Intimate or sanitary goods, hazardous materials, or flammable liquids or gases

7. Sale Items

Only regular priced items may be refunded. Sale items cannot be refunded.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our returns and refunds policy, please contact us at support@quickcart.com.

Order Modifications

At Quickcart, we understand that sometimes changes need to be made to an order after it has been placed. Please review the guidelines below for modifying an order.

1. Changing or Cancelling an Order

If you need to make changes to your order, including modifying items, shipping details, or canceling the order, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team as soon as possible at support@quickcart.com.
  2. Provide your order number and specify the changes you'd like to make.
  3. We will do our best to accommodate your request. However, once the order has been processed or shipped, changes may no longer be possible.

2. Adding Items to an Order

If you'd like to add additional items to an order after it has been placed:

  • Please contact us at support@quickcart.com as soon as possible.
  • If the order hasn't been processed yet, we can update your order to include the additional items.
  • For faster service, you may also consider placing a new order for the additional items.

3. Shipping Address Changes

If you've entered the wrong shipping address or need to update it after placing an order:

  • Contact us immediately at support@quickcart.com with the correct address details.
  • We will update your shipping information if the order has not yet been dispatched.
  • Once an order has shipped, we are unable to modify the shipping address. In such cases, you may need to contact the shipping carrier directly for further assistance.

4. Modifications After Shipping

Unfortunately, once your order has been processed and shipped, modifications are no longer possible. However, you can return the item after receiving it in accordance with our Returns & Refunds Policy.

5. Urgent Changes

If your request is urgent, we recommend contacting our customer support team by phone at 1800 555 9545 for immediate assistance.

6. Processing Time for Modifications

Order modifications typically take up to 24 hours to process. Once confirmed, we will send an updated confirmation email reflecting the changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding order modifications, feel free to reach out to our support team at support@quickcart.com.
